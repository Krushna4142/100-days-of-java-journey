## 🎯 Interview Preparation (Day 29 Level)

**Q1. What are the four pillars of OOP?**  
Encapsulation, Inheritance, Polymorphism, Abstraction.

**Q2. What is the difference between method overloading and overriding?**  
Overloading occurs in the same class with different parameters, while overriding occurs in inheritance when a subclass provides a new implementation.

**Q3. What is the difference between ArrayList and HashSet?**  
ArrayList allows duplicates and maintains order, while HashSet stores unique elements.

**Q4. What is the purpose of encapsulation?**  
To protect data and provide controlled access through methods.

**Q5. Why is exception handling important?**  
It prevents program crashes and ensures smooth program execution.
